About Us

Who We Are

Troop 63 is a Scout-led troop governed by the principles of the Scout Oath and Law, and dedicated to the core aims and methods of scouting. Our troop is committed to providing an engaging outdoor program for youth aged 11-17. We are chartered by the Presbyterian Church in New Providence, New Jersey, and welcome all interested individuals. No prior experience is needed to join our troop.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster personal growth, leadership skills, and a sense of adventure among young scouts. Through a variety of outdoor activities, community service, and leadership opportunities, we aim to create a supportive environment where every scout can thrive and make a positive impact.

Contact Us

If you're interested in learning more about Troop 63 or want to get involved, please reach out to us. We look forward to welcoming new members and sharing the exciting opportunities we have to offer.
