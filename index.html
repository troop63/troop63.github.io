About Troop 63

Troop 63 is based at the Presbyterian Church in New Providence, New Jersey. We invite both boys and girls to participate in our diverse activities. We hold meetings every Tuesday at 7 PM and organize monthly campouts. Our activities include hiking, camping, and other outdoor adventures. As a youth-led troop, we are committed to enhancing our members' experiences through leadership and community service.
